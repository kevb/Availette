Availette

Availette is a simple HTML5 / JQuery plugin for displaying your availability in a calendar. You can specify a google calendar URL from which to take your availability. Availette is perfect for displaying your availability on your website, as a freelancer or a business.

Usage

For the most basic usage, include momentJS, availette.js and availette.css in your HTML. You may also require both momentJS and Availette as AMD modules.

To add the Availette calendar to the page, give it a target like so:

opts = {googleCalendarUrl: 'http://'};
$('#targetdiv').availette(opts);
